This year, Ethics Follies will be performing an as-yet untitled show at the Association of Corporate Counsel's annual meeting in San Antonio!

Educate

Attendees receive two hours of CLE/CPE for attending this fun show with real legal and business ethics issues set to music.

Give Back

A substantial portion of the registration proceeds are donated to the Community Justice Program. Ethics Follies provides over half the CJP's annual operating budget each year. Your Ethics Follies registration provides free legal services to those in need.

Network

Be a part of the valuable networking that goes on each year at the Follies shows, where you are surrounded by ethical decision-makers who can help you grow your organization.
