Recognizing the value of ethics in law and business

Click here to download the rules and nomination form.

The Fifteenth Annual Ethical Life Awards will honor one in-house lawyer or legal department and one private lawyer or law firm that exemplified ideal or exceptionally ethical behavior in the practice of law.

Nominations can be made by anyone with personal knowledge of the nominee’s commitment to ethical conduct. The awards will be presented during the 7 p.m. show's intermission. The awards are presented each year during intermission of the evening performance.

The judges who selected the Ethical Life Awards recipients for 2012 unanimously voted to change the name of the awards to “The Lee Cusenbary Ethical Life Award” in recognition of Lee’s commitment to ethics in the San Antonio Community and his efforts in writing Ethics Follies each year.
